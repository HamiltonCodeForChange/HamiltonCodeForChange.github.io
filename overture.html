About Overture

Get ready for Overture, a exciting day-long hackathon where high school students from all over Hamilton can team up, code, and tackle to head scratching problems! Hosted by Hamilton Code for Change (HCFC), a student-run group, this event gives you the chance to level up your programming skills, learn new things, and explore how technology can help solve real-world problems.


Why you should be there

  • Learning By Doing: Get the opportunity to work on exciting projects that are relevant to the world
  • Team up with others: Wether you're a begginer or an expert, you'l be able to find a team thats right for you
  • Prizes and Food: Who doesn't like free prizes and food!
  • Meet new People: Meet people who are interested in coding just like you!
